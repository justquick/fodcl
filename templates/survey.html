Policy Review Board

Have you heard of the Policy Review Board? Do you know what they do? {{ form.PRB_know }} {{ form.PRB_know.errors }}

The Policy Review Board meets for 2 hours, 4 times a year to supervise the future of the lake. Members consist of Governer appointed representatives of different groups with an interest in the future of the lake, such as the Property Owners Association, and commercial interests in the area. The DNR Lake Management team reports to this Board, and follows their recommendations.
